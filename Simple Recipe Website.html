Pineapple Spam Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 🍍 Pineapple
  • 🍚 Rice (day old, cold)
  • 🥩 Spam
  • 🫘 Soy Sauce
  • 🫛 Vegetables (carrots, green onion, parsley)
  • 🥚Eggs
  • 🧄Garlic
  • 🏺Sesame oil
  • 🧂Salt and Pepper

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Cut pineapple into small cubes.
  2. Prep ingredients (dice and cut vegetables & garlic).
  3. Cut cubed spam into half-inch cubes.
  4. In a wok, saute the garlic and white part of the green onions until golden-brown.
  5. Add in the rice, break it up in the wok, and fry until ready.
  6. Remove from work and set aside.
  7. Add sesame oil, stir fry pineapple and vegetables until ready. Set aside.
  8. Add sesame oil, fry spam cubes with soy sauce until golden brown.
  9. Combine all ingredients and stir fry until ready.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
